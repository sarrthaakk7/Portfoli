Things I Love

// Beyond the classroom — the things that make me, me.

Outside of school, I have a bunch of things I'm really into. Some of them are how I unwind after a long day, some of them keep me sharp, and some of them are just things I can't imagine my life without. Here's a look at what I get up to when I'm not studying.

★ Featured Hobby

🏀
Sport · My #1

Basketball

Basketball is probably the biggest part of my life outside school. I've been playing for years and I absolutely love it — the fast pace, the teamwork, the strategy behind every play. There's something really satisfying about working hard with a team to win a game. It keeps me physically fit and mentally focused, and honestly it's one of the best stress relievers I know.

★ Another Favourite

🎸
Music · Creative outlet

Playing Guitar

I started learning guitar because I've always loved music, and playing it felt like the natural next step. It's a great creative outlet — whenever I'm stressed or need a break, picking up the guitar just helps me reset. I'm still learning, but I really enjoy the process of getting better at it, one chord at a time.
